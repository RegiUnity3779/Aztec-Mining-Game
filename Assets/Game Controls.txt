Controls:
-Up & Down to move about
-Left & Right to rotate
-Spacebar to interact allowing you to breaking rocks, collect ore and floor progression
-Interact with ladders to go down or upstairs
-Enabling the Diamond shaped AutoEquip Mode accessibility feature allows you to play without needing to manually change equipment and focus on exploring.
-Enabling Zen Mode allows you to play a more relaxed game without worrying about stamina.
